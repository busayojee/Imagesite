Dami whose full name is Oluwadamilola is a self-taught artist.

She first discovered her love for art at the age of 7. She recalls the time her parents bought her first set of watercolors and an artist pad, she described the feeling of creating as freedom to express her mind. During that time, she made various patterns which she says helped her mix her numerous designs into one amazing piece she loved. As she went through high school began to be tedious and she never really painted for a while. After high school She began to express her creativity through photography, she went on to learn how to use Photoshop, Lightroom and Adobe Illustrator. After deciding to start painting again she discovered acrylic paint. When asked on why she uses acrylic paint in her art work she answered by describing acrylic paint as “versatile” giving her the creative freedom she desired and decided that she would focus her love for art in that aspect and occasionally trying out new techniques.
